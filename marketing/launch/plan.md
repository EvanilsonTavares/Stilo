# Launch Plan (Brazil, 7 days)

## Offer
- Price: R$ 14.99 / month
- Trial: 7 days full access
- Promise: technical diagnosis + low cost outfit links

## Revenue Mix
- Subscription
- Affiliate links (marketplaces): Mercado Livre, Shopee, Shein, others

## Positioning
- Affordable, technical, direct
- Focus on biotype, color, fit, and low cost pieces

## Funnel
- Content -> DM/WhatsApp -> trial -> payment -> delivery -> affiliate links

## Day by Day
Day 1
- Finalize offer, price, and promise
- Create WhatsApp CTA and a simple landing page (Notion or Linktree)

Day 2
- Build 6-8 short videos (Reels/TikTok/Shorts)
- Create 3 proof examples (before/after or analysis)

Day 3
- Outreach: 100-200 DMs/day
- Target: men 23-45, professionals, gym, executives

Day 4
- Post a full case study (analysis + upgrades + links)
- Go live for 15-20 min: common style mistakes + fixes

Day 5
- Official launch: limited spots for trial users
- One CTA only: send photo + occasion -> diagnosis in 24h

Day 6
- Follow-up on DMs, highlight proof and testimonials

Day 7
- Close first cohort, collect feedback and testimonials

## Content Themes
- 3 mistakes that make you look sloppy (and cheap fixes)
- Complete look under R$ 150-200
- Colors that wash you out vs colors that enhance
- Fit rules for broad shoulders / narrow shoulders

## KPIs
- DMs sent, replies, trials started, paid conversions
- Affiliate clicks and purchases
- Retention after week 1

## Notes
- Always mark affiliate links as such.
- Keep delivery within 24h to maximize conversions.
